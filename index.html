Vision and Mission

Vision

ME­-TA was created to be a resource to existing biometric departments. With an ambition to further develop the role of statisticians and statistical programmers. ME­-TA will help statisticians and programmers increase their effectiveness by encouraging innovative initiatives. ME­-TA strive to make the complicated simpler and transparent.

Mission

Within the traditional roles of programmer and statistician we are a resource on expert level. Together, we have the ambition and the know how to bring a good idea into a daily implemented innovative solution. ME­-TA will help your statisticians and programmers increase their effectiveness in their roles by encouraging best practices and support innovative initiatives.

ME-TA is the innovative CRO that insists to add value through innovation.

Services

Our special focuses are:

Consultancy

We have expertise within biostatistics and sas programming. Through experience in expert roles in pharmaceutical companies, we document the level of expertise that our service provides. As Global Development Team - project statistician at Takeda and as Senior Specialist SAS programmer at Lundbeck, Martin Eeg and Toni Awwad have been recognized as experts within their fields by both Nycomed/Takeda and Lundbeck.

Development Plans

We co-author development plans to maximize the impact of investment with a focus on time and the price re-imbursement negotiations in mind. We use rational and economical sound principles to build the value proposition for market access.

Data Visualization

We program visualization tools to integrate and leverage research and clinical data and transform it into actionable insights, to advance molecular medicine faster for a better health and safety of people.

Reporting

We want to be the preferred provider for reporting of clinical trials, delivering data according to CDISC standards and statistical outputs according to ICH E3. The services require that the processes are described in SOPs and that the systems follow the regulations with regards to system validation.

About

With an ambition to further develop the role of statisticians and statistical programmers, ME­-TA is a resource to your biometric department.

ME­-TA will help your statisticians and programmers increase their effectiveness by encouraging innovative initiatives. We make the complicated simpler and transparent and within the traditional roles of programmer and statistician we have the ambition and the knowhow to change a good idea into an implemented innovative solution.

ME­-TA was founded by Martin Eeg and Toni Awwad, both with a history of expert roles within biometrics in pharmaceutical companies, to provide services within statistical consultancy, statistical reporting and general sas programming.
Combined the partners at ME­-TA have worked for 5 of the Top 10 Pharma companies and 9 of the Danish Pharma companies.
